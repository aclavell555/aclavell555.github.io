About this Dog

The Jack Russell Terrier is a small terrier that has its origins in fox hunting in England. It is principally white-bodied and smooth, rough or broken-coated and can be any colour. Although they are considered to be energetic, athletic and active, Oliver is also cool as a cucumber, preferring others to sweat it out as he watches from beneath a swaying palm tree. Great to take to the beach and beachfront parties, this dog will be the heart of the gathering in no time. He's also CPR-certified and has 500 hours of Yoga.
